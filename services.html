Services

Editing

I offer copyediting and proofreading services for almost any short-form content between 1,000 and 30,000 words. Types of work I will edit include, but is not limited to:

  • Academic papers
  • School Blackboard or Canvas posts
  • Professional reports
  • Resumes/CVs/cover letters
  • Marketing emails
  • Blog posts
  • Any other website content

My editing services include:

  • Line editing: Clarifying meaning by modifying sentence structure and word usage.
  • Copyediting: Fixing grammar, punctuation, and stylistic errors.
  • Proofreading: Final readthrough of entire document to ensure all mistakes are filtered out and narrative flows well.

The content I choose to edit varies on a case-by-case basis. I try to keep my projects under 30,000 words as I do still have a full-time job working with Apple News. However, this limit is not set in stone, so please do inquire if you have a longer project you would like to add some polish to!

Design

I also offer graphic design, web design, and branding services for individuals and small businesses. I can draft, draw, and create almost any graphic design project you can dream of! This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Logos
  • Banners, avatars, and backgrounds
  • Business branding such as business cards, email blasts, and pamphlets
  • Resume/CV/cover letter design
  • Social media icons
  • Twitch, YouTube, and other streaming graphics
  • Exterior and interior book design

There are many different ways the term "graphic design" can be interpreted, so please do not hesitate to query me about your project idea.

Pricing

Pricing for both editing and design services starts at $10 per project, minimum. This price is my flat fee for simple projects such as a document 1,000 words or less, or a small icon you want designed. For larger projects, I am happy to give you a quote depending on the scope of your wants and needs. I generally take payments digitally through Venmo or PayPal, but if this does not work for you, I would be happy to try to accommodate your preferred payment method.
