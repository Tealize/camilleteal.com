Editor and designer with a keen eye for aesthetics and detail.

My name is Camille Teal, and I am a professional content manager working with digital magazines in Apple News+, a position I've been in for over four years. I have a Bachelor of Arts in French from the California State University system, which is how I got my unconventional start in editing. I was brought onto the Apple News team to fill a need for a French-speaking copyeditor, and have worked my way up to managing most of their English- and French-speaking Canadian magazines, in addition to many American titles.

In my spare time, I am a freelancer doing line editing, copyediting, and graphic design. You can see all the services I offer on the services page. Feel free to contact me via email or through any of my social media links. Looking forward to hearing from you!
